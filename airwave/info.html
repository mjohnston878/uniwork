Airwave Festival is a day long music festival which began in 2019. It focuses on a variety genres of rock music such as pop-punk, metal and indie music among others.

Airwave Festival is held in the heart of Leeds city centre, which has taken over the old Slam Dunk site.

If you are over the age of 16 you can attend Airwave Festival. ID must be given to get an over 18 wristband which will be needed for buying alcohol.

Like all outdoor festivals there will be restrictions on what you can bring with you. These are as follows.
  • ID should be brought with you if you wish to consume alcohol.
  • Bags must be a maximum size of an a4 piece of paper.
  • Food and drink are not permitted as there are fasacilites on site.
  • Medication must be labeled with your name as it comes from the doctor.

There are a variety of food stands available, selling as follows
  • Burgers and chips
  • Pizza
  • Sandwiches and Salad
  • Burritos
  • Vegan options

  • Doors open at 12:30
  • Food stands and bars open at 13:00
  • First bands starts at 14:00
  • Food stands and bars stop serving at 21:30
  • Last bands finish at 22:00
  • Kick out at 22:30 sharp
